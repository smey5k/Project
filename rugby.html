Rugby originated in England and is now most popular in the UK and other Commonwealth countries like Australia and New Zealand.​
In rugby, two teams try to kick, carry, or pass a ball over an end (goal) line to score points. Rugby is a contact sport involving a lot of tackling, similar to American football. But whereas American football players wear a lot of padding and hard helmets to protect themselves, rugby players wear very little protection. ​
The most important rugby players are: Gareth Edwards, Martin Johnson, David Campese, Brian O’Driscoll, Dan Carter, Victor Vito, Adam Thomson, Jerome Kaino, Ali Williams, Richie McCaw.​
