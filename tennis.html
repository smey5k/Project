Tennis is an Olympic sport and is played at all levels of society and all ages. The modern game of tennis originated in Birmingham, England in the late 19th century as lawn tennis.​
An estimated one billion people worldwide follow tennis, tuning in from around the world. In tennis, players on both sides of an elongated net try to hit a ball with a racket so that it either goes past their opponents or bounces on their opponents’ side twice to score points.​
The most important players are: Serena Williams, Martina Navratilova, Billie Jean King, Martina Hingis, Helen Wills, Kim Clijsters, Doris Hart, Lindsay Davenport, Molla Mallory, Jennifer Capriati, Victoria Azarenka, Simona Halep, Caroline Wozniacki​.
