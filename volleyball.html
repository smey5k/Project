The game has a following of 900 million people, mostly in North America and Western Europe but also in Asia, Australia, and South America.​
Volleyball involves two teams, each on one side of a raised net, trying to “volley” a ball onto the ground of the opposing team’s side for points. One popular variant of the game, known as beach volleyball, is played on sand with two people on each team, as opposed to regular volleyball, where there are normally six players on each team. ​
